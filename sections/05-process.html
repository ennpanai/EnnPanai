The Innovation

From sacred tree
to world-class textile

Every step of the Enn Panai process is performed by trained women employees in Viruppatchi — creating a fully community-owned value chain where the people who tend the trees also transform them into luxury-grade fibre. Click highlighted steps to see our process in action.

01 Collection

Women harvest naturally fallen fruits — zero mechanical intervention, zero environmental disturbance

02 Peeling

Skilled hands remove the outer skin, preserving the integrity of the fibre beneath

03 Cleaning

Thorough washing and precision sorting separates fine apparel-grade from coarser furnishing fibre

04 Extraction

Proprietary technique separates long-staple fibre with exceptional tensile strength and lustre

▶ View Process
05 Drying

Solar-dried at optimal conditions — no chemical accelerants, no artificial energy consumption

▶ View Process
06 Scouring

Chemical-free retting and desizing prepares fibre for intimate blending with premium cotton

07 Spinning

Spun into yarn at a 90:10 cotton-palmyra blend — achieving silky handle with natural luminosity

▶ View Process
08 Fabric

Woven into apparel-grade fabric — breathable, biodegradable, and incomparably unique in the market

Fine Fibre Stream
Apparel Grade Fabric

The finest palmyra fibres, blended intimately with 90% long-staple cotton, yield a fabric of remarkable softness and natural lustre. Premium sustainable luxury fashion — a fibre story that commands premium pricing in conscious consumer markets worldwide.

Coarse Fibre Stream
Furnishing & Home Décor

Thicker, structurally robust palmyra fibres crafted into premium home furnishing articles — addressing the explosive global demand for natural, biodegradable home textiles commanding 30–40% premium over synthetic alternatives in European markets.
