The Founders

Rooted in the land.
Built by its women.

Enn Panai was not founded in a boardroom — it was born in Viruppatchi, Dindigul and Karadivavi, Palladam where Palmyra palms have shaded the landscape for centuries. Our founding directors are women of this village, who transformed a lifetime of proximity to the palm into a world-first textile innovation.

Pushpa
Co-Founder & Director · Enn Panai Pvt. Ltd.
  • Born and raised in Viruppatchi, Dindigul — surrounded by Palmyra groves since childhood
  • Brings decades of hands-on knowledge of the Palmyra palm's seasonal rhythms, fibre quality, and harvest cycles
  • Pioneer of the proprietary extraction process converting agro-waste fruit fibre into apparel-grade textile
  • Leads the community network of women workers across Viruppatchi and neighbouring villages
Indirani
Co-Founder & Director · Enn Panai Pvt. Ltd.
  • Lifelong resident of Karadivavi village — deeply rooted in the Palmyra ecosystem of Tirupur district
  • Passionate advocate for women's economic independence in rural Tamil Nadu
  • Driving force behind the zero-waste sapling nursery programme completing Enn Panai's spiral economy
  • Instrumental in building the trust, workforce, and supply chain that have made Enn Panai investor-ready
"They did not wait for opportunity. They created it — with their own hands, their village's trees, and the conviction that what grows in the earth can clothe the world."
